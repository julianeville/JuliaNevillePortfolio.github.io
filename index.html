WHAT IS DROP ALL ABOUT?

DROP (Drug Resistance Organisation Program) designed by Julia Neville is an anti â€“ drug program that educates high school students around New Zealand. The harm from drugs and alcohol can be wide-ranging; including injury, disease, personal, social and financial problems; and a reduced quality of life for individuals, their families and communities. DROP aims to educate the youth about these issues although there has been a slight decline in teenage substance abuse it is at this age were the effects and harm caused are far more detrimental to them long term. To run this program DROP requires the following elements to be designed: logo, tote bags, , advertisement poster, information booklets and a drug spike test. DROP has been designed in a way to attract teenagers with the use of imagery drawing people in as well as the bold red colours.
